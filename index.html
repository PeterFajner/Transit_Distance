How far can you get on one bus?

  • Drag the blue dot to set the starting point.
  • This shows buses and CTrains, including 7## and 8## school buses.
  • The Walking Distance slider sets how far you are willing to walk to a stop or between stops, in metres.
  • The Number of Buses selector sets how many buses you can use. The default is one, showing how far you can get on one bus. Zero shows only your location. Two shows you how far you can get on two buses (very slow, expect 20+ second loading times).
